AQUA Cruiser

The aqua cruiser, commanded by Saphyr Blue, Beth's older sister, is a heavy artestry battle ship. Equiped with weapons of mass destroction, no known vessel can withstand its might. With light armor and powerfull engines it is like the sniper of spaceships.

WEAPONS:

HEART BREAKER: A devastating laser beam that pierces right through the enemies core, obliterating their transport in the blink of an eye. When at max capacity this laser can erase small planets from existance.

CUPID: This heavy cannon shoots highly explosive projectiles capable of leveling settlements hole.

AZURE Cruiser

The "Jack of all trates" of the BLUE armada, the support ship, and most likely the main target to take down in a battle. Under command of Beth's younger brother Diamantes Blue.

WEAPONS:

ARTILERY CANNONS: Rapid fire cannons armed to deffend the main turret of the ship.

AZURE TEARS: A barrage of hidrogen based missiles that can easily destroy any swarm tactics, or turn the surface of any planet into molten lava. The possibilities are endless, just like the amount of missiles.

BLUE Cruiser

Beth Blue pride, the one and only. The BLUE cruiser is the tank of the armada. Heavy armor, powerfull shilds, this behemoth of a ship carries a medium sized fleet of interceptors in its bellie (and many other veichles for many tasks). The base of operations for Adamo and beth on their journey to acomplish their objectives. And, perhaps, their love nest.

WEAPONS:

BLASTER CANNONS: Rapid fire laser cannons, the old, default and reliable turret for almost all ships in the BLUE armada

FEELING BLUE: A small EMP that slows enemies and turn their shilds down, making them vulnerable.

TEARFULL SWARM: Fleet of fighters ready to defend the vessel. capable pilots will defend the cruiser with their very lives.

TWIN HEARTS: The double core of the ship provide both defense and fire power, overcharging its barriers and cannons. And sometimes blowing the engines.
